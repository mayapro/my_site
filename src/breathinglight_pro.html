Breathing Light

BreathingLight is a lamp for people who need a little help falling asleep.

With a tender light and a "lung" that swells and falls in a relaxing rhythm that simulate breathing it is easier to get into a mind sate of sleep.

The breathing act was achieved with an old computer fan that is controlled with an Arduino chip and the wooden part is made out of citrus trunk.
