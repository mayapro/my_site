BlindWatch

The blindWatch is representing a thought about time and what are watches for in the 21st century.
Do we really need them? Is being a jewelry or a status symbol is all that is left for them? Could they carry another essence?

The blindWatch can be wear from both side, the brass one allows the user to hear the clock ticking and the time passing but wouldnâ€™t tell the hour nor the minute. The 3d printed side reveals the watch hands but they are in the same length from both of their sides and the hour can be read at a 6 hours different and the minute at a 30 minutes different.

The clock was designed in SolidWorks, printed in 3d half with vax then casted brass and half with polymeric material. The hands are aluminum that was cut in laser and the watch straps are Leather.
